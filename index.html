What is it?

FantaEurovision stands for "Fantasy Eurovision". It's a game that takes the Eurovision experience to a whole new level.

The rules are very simple: Choose 4 Eurovision countries as members of your team. Based on what your team members will do during the Eurovision Song Contest performance, you will gain or lose points.

How do I join?

You need to follow these four steps!

Choose your team

Select your four players (and an additional Announcer)

Register

Choose a name and select your team in the registration form.

Share

Create/select a League if you want to challenge your friends!

Earn points!

Watch Eurovision Song Contest and cross your fingers!
