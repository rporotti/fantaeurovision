Announcer scoring:
If your FantaAnnouncer country (jury + televote) gives points to 1 or more countries in your team during the final, then you get the same amount of fantapoints multiplied by 10! But, if the FantaAnnouncer country is an "ally" of one of your Team Members, you don't get any fantapoints from that country! "Allies" are countries that are more likely to vote for each other. They're the top 3 countries that an Announcer has given the most points to, in the last 10 years. You can find the full list here.

Example: I choose Albania as Announcer, and my Team Members are Australia, Italy, Belgium and Finland. Then, if Albania gives 12 points to Australia, 8 to Italy, 2 to Belgium and 0 to Finland I will get:

- 120 Fantapoints for Australia (12 points x 10)

- 0 Fantapoints for Italy (Albania is an ally of Italy!)

- 20 Fantapoints for Belgium (2 points x 10)

- 0 Fantapoints for Finland
